Design a modern, premium SaaS landing page for PinFlow — an AI-powered Pinterest content automation tool. The product turns any product URL into 30 days of scheduled Pinterest pins in 5 minutes. Target audience is affiliate marketers, Shopify store owners, and small marketing agencies.

BRAND IDENTITY
Name: PinFlow
Tagline: "Turn any product link into Pinterest content that sells for months — in 5 minutes."
Personality: Clean, confident, quietly premium. Think Linear meets Notion meets a boutique agency site. Not loud. Not startup-bro. Sophisticated but approachable.
Color Palette:

Background: #0A0A0F (near black, deep space feel)
Primary accent: #E60023 (Pinterest red, used sparingly for CTAs only)
Secondary accent: #FF6B9D (soft pink, for gradients and glow effects)
Text primary: #F5F5F7 (near white)
Text secondary: #8A8A9A (muted grey for subtext)
Card backgrounds: #13131A with 1px border #1E1E2E
Gradient accent: linear-gradient(135deg, #E60023, #FF6B9D)

Typography:

Headings: Inter or Geist, weight 700-800, letter spacing -0.02em
Body: Inter, weight 400, line height 1.7
Accent labels: uppercase, weight 600, letter spacing 0.1em, size 11px

GLOBAL DESIGN DETAILS

Rounded corners: 12-16px cards, 999px pills and buttons
Generous section padding, nothing cramped, lots of breathing room
Subtle noise texture on background at 3% opacity for depth
Smooth hover states all interactive elements
Red glow shadow on all primary buttons: 0 0 20px rgba(230,0,35,0.3)
Mobile responsive: hero stacks vertically, stats strip scrolls horizontally, pricing cards stack single column, bento grid collapses to single column
Sections fade in on scroll with subtle upward translate animation
